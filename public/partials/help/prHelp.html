Record a new PR

Record a new PR by selecting an exercise from the left most drop down box. Set the date, amount (in weight, time, rounds, or distance) and select a unit of measure (Lbs, Yards, Count, ect.). Click save to record your PR.

If you have already recorded a PR for the selected exercise, a new History record will be added, allowing you to track your progress.
